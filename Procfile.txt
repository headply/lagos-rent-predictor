
### Key Steps to Complete the `Procfile`

1.  **File Naming:** Ensure the file is named exactly **`Procfile`** (capital P, no extension).
2.  **Location:** Place it in the root directory of your project (where `app.py`, `index.html`, and `requirements.txt` are located).
